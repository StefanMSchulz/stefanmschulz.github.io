Hi, I'm Stefan.

I am pursuing my Master's in Computer Science at the University of Bonn, Germany. Currently, I am spending a semester abroad at Aalto University in Finland and I am loving the experience. At the time of writing, however, my time here is coming to an end but I experienced so many great things, met new people, made new friends and memories that will stay with me forever. Maybe I will use this website also to write a little about my experiences here in Finland, who knows...

Throughout my bachelor studies I have focused on Machine Learning and Robotics. Specifically Computer Vision has been a huge part of my time as an undergrad. Mainly, I am interested in applying Machine Learning to provide good to the community. This is why I decided to write my bachelor thesis on "Predicting AMD Stage and Visual Function from Drusen Ultrastruture". Age-related macular degeneration (AMD) is a disease that has no cure yet but affect many millions of people all around the globe. To me this is a major factor that I considered when doing my thesis, it was meant to be something meaningful. I am striving to get better, learn more and hopefully, one day, I will be able to help find a cure to a disease. Therefore, specializing even further in the domain of Machine Learning and Computer Vision/Robotics was a no-brainer and I am happy to be able to do what I want.
